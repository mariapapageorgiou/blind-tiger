Be Prepared

Play By The Rules

No smoking of any kind (vaping included) is allowed. Phonecalls or any other use of an electronic device (yes, we are talking about your social media addiction) is not permitted. Only in case of an emergency and step outside please.

Service animals are welcome to join. The treats are on us.

Sitting will be assigned as first come first served. In addition, due to the current COVID restrictions enforced by the city, any party larger than 5 will have to go home. Wait times might be longer than expected.

We have a zero-tolerance policy against harassment, bullying and discrimination in place. Respect the LGBTQ community, all religions, all sexes, all races. ALL!

Non-compliance with the rules might lead to your early exit and say bye bye to your Mary Pickford cocktail that just arrived.

Your Vibe

21+ is the magic number to enter (age restrictions apply).

Cocktail attire and period attire is encouraged. No hats, hoodies, sweatpants, flip-flops, or any sort of beach attire. In three words: Dress to impress. In two: Upscale Casual.

Let's pretend we are still in the prohibition era and try to keep your conversations at a reasonable level. Feel free to dance your shoes off.

If you are a smoker, you can step outside to our smoking area. We promise not to judge.

Social Media addicts, this is not the place for you. Please remove yourselves from our property at your own convenience.

Drink responsibly!

Seriously, NEVER drink and drive.
