Play By The Rules

We are unavailable to accept reservations at the moment for any occasion.

No smoking of any kind (vaping included) is allowed inside the Blind Tiger. Phonecalls or any other use of an electronic device (yes, we are talking about your social media addiction) is not permitted. Only in case of an emergency and step outside please. No compliance by the rules might lead to your early exit and say bye bye to your Mary Pickford cocktail that just arrived.

Service animals are welcome to join you. The treats are on us.

Sitting will be assigned as first come first serve and based on your party size. In addition, due to the current COVID restrictions enforced by the city, any party larger than 10 will have to go home (we have "eyes" everywhere). Wait times might be longer than expected.

We have a zero-tolerance policy against harassment, bullying and discrimination in place. Respect the LGBTQ (rainbow flag here) community, all religions, all sexes, all races. ALL! No compliance by the rules will lead to a permanent ban.
